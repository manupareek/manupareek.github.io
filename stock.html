Ticker: MSFT

Ticker: TWTR

Despite a consensus annual EBITDA growth forecast of over 40% for the next 3 years, the stock price currently implies a much lower growth rate of around 20%. Also, given the recent decline in stock price over the past year, TWTR is attractively positioned for a potential acquisition.

Ticker: GOOG

Despite a healthy move in in the stock over the past year, there is still room for upside. I expect the company to continue to trend higher on above consensus earnings performance. Furthermore, the advent of and wider adoption of ad-tech will lead to a greater consolidation of advertiser budgets towards large advertising networks such as GOOG.
