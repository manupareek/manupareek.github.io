MBA Candidate - Kellogg School of Management


Summary

I am a second year MBA student at the Kellogg School of Management. I have over 8 years of experience across McKinsey, Goldman Sachs and starting two online retail companies in India. I am passionate about the consumer internet space and want to build a long term career in the industry.

I started my career at McKinsey in 2007 as a part of the firm's corporate finance practice. I worked on projects spanning across telecom, steel, pharma and real estate with the functional focus always being finance. I really enjoyed the analytical rigor of the work and that prompted me to take the logical next step into banking.

I joined Goldman Sachs in mid-2010 as an equity analyst. I got an opportunity to cover the US Internet sector which gave me a chance to learn about the consumer technology landscape. I was immediately inspired by the way technology was shaping the consumer experience and creating value, and started looking for ways to be a bigger part of it.

I started two online retail businesses in India both dealing in apparel. My first venture was CandidlyCouture.com which operated in the niche women's western segment. I left the company and my job at Goldman Sachs and started Clickroo.com. Clickroo is a mens casual fashion focused website based out of Kolkata. We have a vertically integrated operation with in-house design and manufacturing capabilities. I was involved in getting the company from idea to launch stage and I led the entire digital marketing for the first year. However I have now reduced my involvement to weekends and vacations and only work on high level strategic initiatives.

While I was getting all these experiences, the core motivation was my strong passion for technology and my intent to work in the space in the medium term before returning to entrepreneurship. I came to Kellogg to gain specific skills in leadership, and strategy, which I believe will help me as I make this career transition. After my MBA, I wish to work in a consumer internet company in the online advertising space, in a business side role.
